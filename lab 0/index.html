About VIT-AP

VIT-AP University, located in Amaravati, Andhra Pradesh, is known for high-quality education, innovation, modern infrastructure, and strong industry collaboration. The campus encourages students to explore academics, research, and personal development.

Programs Offered

  • B.Tech – CSE, ECE, Mechanical
  • M.Tech & MBA Programs
  • B.Sc, BBA, BA, and other professional courses
  • Ph.D. Programs in Engineering & Sciences

Campus Life

The university provides modern labs, sports facilities, clubs, festivals, hackathons, hostels, and a vibrant student ecosystem that promotes learning beyond academics.
